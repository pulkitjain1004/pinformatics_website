RECRUITMENT for a User Study on Information Privacy

Interested in data science and information privacy?

Participate in a research study on data and information privacy

Howdy!

You are invited to participate in a research study on data and information privacy.

WHEN: Monday (8/7) Morning 10am

The goal of this project is to study how information disclosure can (1) facilitate better replicable decisions while also (2) increasing privacy by only disclosing information that is required when needed. The study will involve the use of visualization software to answer a series of questions based on the data you see on the screen. No particular expertise is required to participate. To qualify for the study, you must be at least 18 years of age.

The entire study will last approximately 60 minutes, but could potentially take up to 90 minutes. You will receive a $15 Amazon gift card plus the top performer will receive an additional $35 Amazon gift card.

Please click to schedule your appointment in our google form.

For additional information regarding the study please contact Debra Tan at debra.tan@tamu.edu.

The study will take place at Room 104, Blocker Building, 155 Ireland St on the Texas A&M University campus.
