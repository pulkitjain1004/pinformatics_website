Disclosure Mode

[Warning: do not refresh your web browser before the end of the user study. ]

Select a disclosure mode:





In Full Disclosure No Intervention mode, there's no intervention, no icon or frequency. This is the baseline for reference.
In Full Disclosure mode, all the cells are visible.
In Full Disclosure For Different Cells mode, only different cells in a pair are visible. Dash lines represent the identical text in the pair.
In Paritial Disclosure mode, only the minimum differences are visible at start. However, you can click on the cells which are partially visible, then the cells pair will be shown.
In Minimum Disclosure Non-clickable mode, only positions of differences are shown.
