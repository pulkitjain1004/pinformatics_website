HIPAA Training

CITI Training

https://www.citiprogram.org/

  • Register and create new user account
  • Go to “Click here to affiliate with another institution” and use Texas A&M University as the institution.
  • Go to “Add a course or Update Learner Groups” and answer following questions as:
    • You want to check only the “Group 1: Biomedical Research Investigators and Key Personnel ” item.
    • For other mandatory questions: “Not at this time”
  • When you open the course page, you will see the list of modules like this:
    • Belmont Report and CITI Course Introduction (ID: 1127)
    • History and Ethics of Human Subjects Research (ID: 498)
    • Basic Institutional Review Board (IRB) Regulations and Review Process (ID: 2)
    • Informed Consent (ID: 3)
    • Social and Behavioral Research (SBR) for Biomedical Researchers (ID: 4)
    • Records-Based Research (ID: 5)
    • Genetic Research in Human Populations (ID: 6)
    • Populations in Research Requiring Additional Considerations and/or Protections (ID: 16680)
    • Vulnerable Subjects – Research Involving Prisoners (ID: 8)
    • Vulnerable Subjects – Research Involving Children (ID: 9)
    • Vulnerable Subjects – Research Involving Pregnant Women, Human Fetuses, and Neonates (ID: 10)
    • International Studies (ID: 971)
    • Students in Research (ID: 1321)
    • FDA-Regulated Research (ID: 12)
    • Research and HIPAA Privacy Protections (ID: 14)
    • Conflicts of Interest in Research Involving Human Subjects (ID: 488)
    • Avoiding Group Harms – U.S. Research Perspectives (ID: 14080)
    • Texas A&M University (ID: 999)
  • Complete the training and submit PDF to your supervisor

You can also find internal HIPAA Training in HR (SSO)

You can find more guidance for these courses at the following site.
http://sph.tamhsc.edu/research/hipaa/training.html
